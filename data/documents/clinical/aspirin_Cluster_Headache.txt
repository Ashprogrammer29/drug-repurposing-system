Trial: NCT00550446
Title: A Phase 2 Study For Patients With A Physician's Diagnosis Of Rheumatoid Arthritis
Phase: PHASE2
Status: COMPLETED
Summary: The purpose of this study is to determine the effectiveness and safety, over 6 months, of 5 doses of CP-690,550 for the treatment of adults with active rheumatoid arthritis. Five out of seven subjects will receive CP-690,550. One out of seven will receive adalimumab (Humira®) and one out of seven will only receive inactive substances (placebo.)
