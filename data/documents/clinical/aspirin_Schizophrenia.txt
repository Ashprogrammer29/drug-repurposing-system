Trial: NCT07129824
Title: Relationship Between Diet and Gastritis
Phase: NA
Status: NOT_YET_RECRUITING
Summary: Chronic gastritis (CG) is a prevalent and persistent inflammatory condition that affects the gastric mucosa, carrying a significant global burden. Although CG may remain asymptomatic in some cases, many patients experience gastrointestinal (GI) symptoms such as epigastric pain, bloating, nausea, vomiting, and appetite loss.

Diet plays a crucial role in both the development and progression of CG.
