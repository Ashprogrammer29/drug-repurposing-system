Title: Endpoint Calibration for a Phase 2 Study of Lisinopril in Multiple Sclerosis
Phase: PHASE2
Status: UNKNOWN
Summary: This study aims to calibrate the Multiple Sclerosis Functional Composite (MSFC) for home implementation in a phase 2 trial of lisinopril in multiple sclerosis. In this initial stage, participants are required to travel to the study site one day a week for three weeks to complete the MSFC.
