Title: Efficacy And Safety Of Pregabalin For Pain Following Total Knee Replacement
Phase: PHASE3
Status: COMPLETED
Summary: Pregabalin added to the standard of care with dosing starting preoperatively and continuing for up to 6 weeks post surgery will decrease the intensity of post-operative pain following total knee replacement.
