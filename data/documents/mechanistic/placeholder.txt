Metformin activates AMP-activated protein kinase (AMPK) pathway.
AMPK activation is associated with neuroprotective effects in neurodegeneration.
Metformin reduces mTOR signaling which is implicated in Alzheimer's disease pathology.
AMPK activation reduces tau phosphorylation and amyloid beta production.
Biological pathway: AMPK-mTOR-tau axis relevant to neurodegeneration.
Drug mechanism: insulin sensitizer with pleiotropic effects on neuroinflammation.
